--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About With this public exercise, we hope to honor the extraordinary contribution of Dr. Lorgia García Peña in two ways: First by defending and helping the public understand the Ethnic Studies that García Peña has worked hard to help build herself. This is the goal of our Roundtable; and Second, by celebrating, re-centering, and bringing a discussion of her work to wider audiences. This is the goal of our LorgiaFest. The project was conceived of and brought to fruition by Raj Chetty (St. John's University) and Katerina Gonzalez Seligmann (Emerson College), in collaboration with Alex Gil (Columbia University).
