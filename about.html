--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About Welcome. "Ethnic Studies Rise" is a public humanities effort to honor the extraordinary contributions of the scholar Dr. Lorgia García Peña. First by defending and helping the public understand the Ethnic Studies that García Peña has worked hard to help build throughout her distinguished career. This is the goal of our Roundtable; and Second, by celebrating, re-centering, and bringing a discussion of her work to wider audiences. This is the goal of our LorgiaFest.
