--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About Welcome. "Ethnic Studies Rise" is a public humanities effort to honor the extraordinary contributions of scholar Dr. Lorgia García Peña: First, by defending and helping the public understand the Ethnic Studies that García Peña has worked hard to help build throughout her distinguished career—this is the goal of our Roundtable; and Second, by celebrating, re-centering, and bringing a discussion of her work to wider audiences—this is the goal of our LorgiaFest. We hope that in honoring Dr. García Peña, we also recognize and honor in their own right all of those scholars who have encountered resistance and retaliation for their timely work in the United States, and to the students who need them and fight daily to belong.
